A Custom Organic Glow for Raena O'Neil of Lymphatic Organic

Costa Mesa, California

When your client is the founder of a wellness brand, the tan has to live up to her standards — clean, natural, and absolutely seamless. I recently had the joy of creating a custom organic airbrush tan for Raena O'Neil, founder of Lymphatic Organic in Costa Mesa, ahead of her photoshoot.

Raena's world is all about what goes on and in the body, so we used an organic airbrush solution and built her color in light, even layers — customized to her skin tone and the golden, sun-kissed depth she wanted on camera. The goal with every spray tan I do is the same: color that looks like you just returned from a week somewhere beautiful, with zero streaks, zero orange, and a flawless fade.

The proof is in the photos — from a dramatic black tulle gown to a feathered blush mini, her glow photographed beautifully in every look and every lighting setup. A great airbrush tan doesn't announce itself; it just makes everything else look better.

All of my spray tan services are mobile and on-location, so your glow comes to you — before your event, your shoot, or your vacation.
